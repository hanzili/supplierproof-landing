Greenhouse gas emissions

Question

Question: What were Acme’s Scope 1 and Scope 2 GHG emissions for FY2024?

Answer

Answer: Scope 2 electricity is 1,100 tCO2e; Scope 1 natural gas is 413 tCO2e; refrigerants are missing.

Proof

Proof: 12-month electricity bill, 12-month natural gas bill, and metadata reporting year FY2024.

Method

Calculated from utility bills using EPA emission factors. Electricity: 2,850,000 kWh × 0.000386 tCO2e/kWh. Natural gas: 78,000 therms × 0.005302 tCO2e/therm.

Boundary: Scope 1 is incomplete — refrigerant data not provided. These estimates use EPA published emission factors and are suitable for THESIS reporting. This is not a verified GHG inventory, audit, assurance statement, or SBTi target-setting analysis. For verified carbon accounting or SBTi target-setting, consult a carbon accounting specialist.
