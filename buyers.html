Scope boundaries

Clear first-pass checks, without pretending to be an auditor.

SupplierProof makes source evidence easier to review. Buyer retains final decision and supplier performance judgment.

We check

  • Completeness against the request
  • Expiration dates and reporting years
  • Facility, legal entity, or product-scope mismatch
  • Unsupported claims and missing attachments
  • Nonresponsive suppliers and follow-up owners

We do not

  • Certify supplier claims
  • Score or rank suppliers
  • Provide legal opinions
  • Replace buyer approval
  • Guarantee document authenticity unless separately agreed
