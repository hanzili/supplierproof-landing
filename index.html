One workflow, two entry points

Supplier documentation support for buyers and suppliers.

SupplierProof helps buyers and suppliers turn messy ESG and compliance documentation requests into clear next actions. Not another ESG platform. Not an audit. Not a certification.

Start from either side: a buyer can sponsor support for selected suppliers, or a supplier can use SupplierProof to respond better to customer requests. Either way, the output is clear status, missing-info, and next actions.

One workflow, different incentives

Buyers need clarity. Suppliers need control. The system should remember the work.

SupplierProof sits in the middle: support first, triage second, reusable memory over time. A buyer can initiate support for selected suppliers, or a supplier can initiate support to respond to customer requests. The common trigger is customer pressure and the common asset is reusable documentation memory.

1

Buyer gets operational clarity

Ready, missing, mismatched, expired, or awaiting escalation — with next actions your team can review.

2

Supplier gets controlled support

Private prep and redactions make the workflow feel like support, not surveillance.

3

Memory compounds over time

Approved answers, documents, gaps, and deadlines become reusable context for future ESG and compliance requests.

Testing with early teams

Have one ESG or compliance request stuck across suppliers?

Send one request and a small supplier sample. We classify what is ready, missing, mismatched, expired, or nonresponsive, then give your team a clear next-action and escalation view.
