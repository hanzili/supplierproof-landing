Will my brand customers know I used AI?

They'll see the same answers, cited to the same files, signed by you. AI drafts; you approve. Faster, not different.

What if a brand uses a vendor portal, not email?

Both work. Paste portal questions in; we extract them the same way. Ship as ZIP or paste each answer back.

What about confidential info?

Disclosure rules per brand. KIND: no raw-material supplier names. Annie's: no cost data. We auto-redact and show you exactly what we removed.

Can my team use this together?

Yes — multiple seats, role-based access, full audit log on Growth and Scale.

How is this different from a shared Drive?

Drive doesn't watch certs expire, auto-fill KIND's Q3, or redact per-brand rules. Drive is filing — SupplierProof is the system around the filing.

Do my raw-material suppliers need an account?

No. One-time magic link, drop the file, done. Biggest reason they actually reply on time.

What does the first 30 days look like?

Day 1: bring 3 past brand packets, we seed overnight. Week 1: next brand RFI auto-drafts ~50%. Week 4: packets ship in an afternoon, not a fortnight.
