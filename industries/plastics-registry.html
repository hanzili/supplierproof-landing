What SupplierProof helps manage

  1. Translate reporting requirements into the exact data fields needed from internal teams and partners.
  2. Track which facilities, franchisees, vendors, or service providers need to respond.
  3. Check for missing resin categories, product volumes, waste quantities, reporting years, and source support.
  4. Draft follow-ups for incomplete or inconsistent data.
  5. Prepare a submission support packet with sources, assumptions, and open gaps.

Best fit

Companies that know plastics reporting is coming, but still need an operating workflow for collecting complete data from many owners and partners.

Common reporting bottlenecks

Many data owners

Data may sit with facilities, procurement, packaging teams, franchisees, vendors, or waste service providers.

Category mapping

Resin, product, stream, and market categories need to match the reporting format.

Evidence support

Teams need sources for quantities, assumptions, service-provider data, and facility-level estimates.

Deadline pressure

The workflow needs status, reminders, and escalation before reporting deadlines.
