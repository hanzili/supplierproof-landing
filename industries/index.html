How we usually start

  1. Start with one live or recent request, form, checklist, or spreadsheet.
  2. Map what evidence is needed and what is already available.
  3. Flag missing, expired, unsupported, or inconsistent data.
  4. Draft the follow-ups and prepare a response package for your team to review.
