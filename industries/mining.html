Mining assurance evidence

One set of site evidence often has to support several mining standards.

SupplierProof helps sustainability, EHS, and assurance teams organize source evidence, owner follow-ups, and support files across voluntary mining standards and external verification reviews.

Review one assurance workflowRedacted protocol or evidence checklist welcome.

What SupplierProof helps manage

  1. Map evidence requirements across TSM, ICMM-aligned standards, Copper Mark/IRMA-like frameworks, and internal protocols.
  2. Identify the source files, site owners, expiry dates, and review status for each evidence item.
  3. Flag gaps before external verification or management review.
  4. Draft site-owner follow-ups for missing logs, procedures, invoices, policies, or sign-offs.
  5. Prepare a support packet that reviewers can trace back to source evidence.

Best fit

Teams that already collect sustainability and EHS evidence, but still rely on spreadsheets, SharePoint folders, email, and manual framework mapping to prepare for assurance.

Common evidence bottlenecks

Framework overlap

Similar proof is requested in different formats across standards and assurance programs.

Site-level ownership

Evidence comes from many sites and owners, each with different documentation habits.

Source support

External verification needs traceable logs, invoices, procedures, and source files, not just explanations.

Manual status tracking

Owners, sources, expiry dates, and assurance status are often tracked in spreadsheets.
