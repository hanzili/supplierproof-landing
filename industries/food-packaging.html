Food & packaging customer requests

Customer sustainability requests are messy. We help get them ready for review.

When retailers or major customers ask for CDP, Walmart Gigaton, EcoVadis, THESIS-like assessments, packaging claims, sourcing proof, or certificates, SupplierProof helps organize the evidence and chase what is missing.

Review one customer requestStart with one redacted questionnaire, spreadsheet, or email.

What SupplierProof helps manage

  1. Break down the customer request into answerable evidence items.
  2. Identify which certificates, supplier declarations, packaging specs, utility data, or sourcing proof are needed.
  3. Flag missing, expired, unsupported, or mismatched evidence.
  4. Draft internal and supplier follow-ups for the exact missing information.
  5. Prepare an approval-ready response package with caveats and attachment checklist.

Best fit

Mid-market food, ingredient, and packaging suppliers that sell to large customers but do not have a large ESG/compliance operations team.

Requests we can help with

Customer questionnaires

CDP, EcoVadis, Walmart Gigaton, THESIS-like assessments, and retailer scorecards.

Packaging proof

Recycled content, recyclability, fiber sourcing, material specs, and supplier declarations.

Certificates

GFSI/SQF/BRCGS, RSPO/FSC, Sedex/SMETA, insurance, facility, and product-level documents.

Response quality

Units, years, methodology, source coverage, expired files, and unsupported claims.
