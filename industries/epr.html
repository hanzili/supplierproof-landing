EPR / producer responsibility reporting

Producer reporting gets stuck in missing fields, reminders, and fee-impacting corrections.

SupplierProof helps stewardship and recycling programs manage the admin around producer/member submissions: product categories, sales volume, weights, province or region, signatures, supporting files, and finance handoff.

Review one reporting workflowBlank or redacted templates are enough to start.

What SupplierProof helps manage

  1. Track which producers or members have submitted, missed, or partially completed reports.
  2. Check reports for missing product type, volume, weight, region/province, and required signatures.
  3. Draft clear follow-ups for incomplete or inconsistent submissions.
  4. Support multilingual explanations when members need help understanding the request.
  5. Prepare a clean status summary for operations, finance, and audit review.

Best fit

Programs where member reports affect fees, compliance, recycling obligations, or finance reconciliation, and where the follow-up work still happens across PDFs, spreadsheets, email, and custom portals.

Common reporting bottlenecks

Incomplete submissions

Members skip required fields, signatures, attachments, or reporting periods.

Correction loops

Teams spend time writing “page 9 is missing” or “weight by province is incomplete” emails.

Finance risk

Bad product, volume, or weight data can affect member fees and revenue recognition.

Language barriers

International and bilingual members need clearer explanations than a generic PDF can provide.
